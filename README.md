üìä Dashboard Features & Key Metrics
The dashboard is structured to provide a detailed view of hotel performance, tracking industry-standard Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

Revenue Management: Analysis of Total Revenue, Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR), and Average Daily Rate (ADR).

Occupancy & Utilization: Tracking Occupancy Rate, Total Rooms Booked, and Cancellation Rates.

Customer & Booking Insights: Visualization of booking trends over time, segmentation of revenue by Room Type, and analysis of Lead Time.

Financial Health: Monitoring Net Profit, identifying high-cost areas, and benchmarking profitability across different segments (e.g., corporate vs. leisure).

Time Series Analysis: Trends showing daily, weekly, or monthly performance to identify seasonality and forecast demand.
